EMPOWERING WELLNESS

Curriculum for Underweight Children

Nutrient-Rich Diet:

Consume a balanced diet rich in proteins, healthy fats, and carbohydrates. Include nutrient-dense foods like nuts, seeds, and lean proteins in each meal.

Strength Training Exercises:

Engage in strength training exercises at least three times a week to build muscle mass. Include activities like weightlifting, resistance training, or bodyweight exercises.

Protein Supplements:

Consider incorporating protein supplements after consulting with a nutritionist or healthcare professional.

Monitor Physical Activity:

Track daily physical activity to ensure a healthy balance between calorie expenditure and intake.

Nutritional Counseling:

Seek guidance from a nutritionist for personalized dietary advice and meal planning.

Adequate Rest:

Ensure sufficient sleep for proper recovery and muscle growth.

Curriculum for Correct weight Children

Nutrition:

Maintain a well-balanced diet that includes a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

Portion Control:

Learn about appropriate portion sizes to avoid overeating.

Regular Physical Activity:

Engage in at least 60 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week.

Mind-Body Exercises:

Include activities like yoga and meditation for both physical and mental well-being.

Hydration:

Drink an adequate amount of water daily to support overall health.

Variety in Exercise:

Explore a mix of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility workouts.

Lifestyle Choices:

Make healthy lifestyle choices, including limiting screen time and getting outdoors regularly.

Curriculum for Overweight Children

Healthy Eating Habits:

Focus on a balanced diet with a reduction in processed foods, sugary drinks, and high-fat snacks.

Regular Physical Activity:

Engage in at least 60 minutes of moderate to vigorous physical activity most days of the week.

Fun Workouts:

Choose enjoyable activities like dancing, cycling, or swimming to make exercise engaging.

Family Involvement:

Encourage family activities that promote physical fitness, fostering a supportive environment.

Nutritional Education:

Attend sessions to understand the nutritional value of foods and make informed choices.

Limit Sedentary Time:

Reduce screen time and replace it with outdoor play or other physical activities.

Goal Setting:

Set achievable goals for weight loss and monitor progress regularly.
